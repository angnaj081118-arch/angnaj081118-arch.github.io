TTT Saturday Special

TTT is wondering what makes people run the moment they see her. She doesn't think she looks scary, so it must be something else that's startling them. Why do horror movies always use TVs as a motif? Maybe it's because there's a type of character that can naturally appear on-screen, talk to whoever's on the other side, and not raise any alarm. A weather reporter? A shopping channel host? Or maybe she could pretend to be a super hacker? As she trails along the wires, a video order system at a drive-in diner catches her eye. "This is it! A drive-in diner server! Friendly pink uniform, roller skates weaving through parked cars, decadent desserts, and always serving from the other side of the glass!" "I'll have a Big Max." "One extra-large milkshake." "A Big Max, no mayo." Well, at least this time, no one is running away screaming.
